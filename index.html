Overview

Retrowave deer is a collection of 7,777 NFTs with iconic items from the 80s. Owning a retrowave deer NFT provides you with access to the next-gen nft market and exclusive merch. Every deer is unique with varying traits created by a generative algorithm and registered on the Ethereum blockchain.

Our team is aiming to launch a fully-functional NFT marketplace. 5% of collection sales will be used to increase the price floor of the whole collection (aka buyback). Moreover, team has plans on running synthwave music channel on Youtube and branded merch.
